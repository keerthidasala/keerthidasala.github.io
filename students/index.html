Ph.D Students

Junjie Wu

Junjie obtained her bachelor’s degree in Telecommunication Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. After that, she spent two and a half years at Watech Electronics as a validation engineer for RF devices. Her current research interest is wireless communication systems, with a particular emphasis on addressing fundamental challenges in mobile THz communication. Moreover, Junjie enjoys exploring new culinary recipes and playing with her cat.
